If you recommend us to your two friends you can get a discount of up to 20% off and your friends a gift card.

About us

Our mission is to keep the nutritional value of the spent coffee grounds by upcycling it into food products. We want to be a part of sustainable food transition that doesn’t leave any resources behind, in order to secure the future of coffee and protect the environment from degradation.
